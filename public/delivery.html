Exchange Policy

Our standard return policy for anything you buy from us is 35 days. This applies to online purchases, except in case of sale items. You have 14 days for sale items bought online, unless otherwise stated. If the item is unused, in its original packaging and accompanied by a valid proof of purchase, we’ll offer an exchange in our coin stores (store returns only) or a full refund. This does not affect your statutory rights. If you have an issue with the quality of a product purchased from us, please get in touch and contact us.

Exchanging Items

If items are unused or in the same condition as you bought them in, you are entitled to exchange your coin with another that we have in store. If the product you want costs less than the one you are returning, we are able to refund you for remainder of the amount. If the item you want costs more than the one you are returning, you will need to pay the difference. If you are considering swapping your coin for another, please view our products. If you have any questions for our customer servie team, please contact us. If you prefer to speak on the phone, please call 07111 111111.

How can I pay for an order?

You can use any of the payment types listed below to pay for your order. We take your security very seriously, therefore your details are safe with us.

We also take fraud very seriously, so all credit and debit card holders are subject to validation and authorisation by both of us and the card issuer. To better protect our customers when making online payments you may be presented with 3D secure security measures when paying. This is dependent on your bank issuer and may be require you to enter a password or get a security code via email or phone.

If you don't want to save your payment details to your account, you can also enter them when you get to checkout. If there are no saved payment details, you'll be asked to enter them when you are on the checkout page.

At the moment, we do not have any buy now, pay later payment options.
